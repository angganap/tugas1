A Tribute For Didi Kempot

Didi Kempot was an Indonesian singer and songwriter in the campursari style.

Timeline

1966 Didi was born in Surakarta, the son of Ranto Edi Gudel was comedian and Mamiek Prakoso was also comedian.
1984 Didi Kempot started working as a street busker in his hometown, Surakarta.
1993 Didi left for the Netherlands. When he arrived, Didi was surprised that many memorized the single "Cidro".
